Introduction

Jaylub (Jakub Karel Jaros), who was born on September 10, 2009 in the small town of Rakovník, lives in the small village of Kolešovice.
He is known for his smell and greasy hair, which he got at the age of 10 because he does not shower and does not use deodorant. His hobbies include, for example, playing football for the team Chrášťany, where a well-known person Miloš Jelínek also played, also known as "krivonozka", or playing the old version of the game Minecraft, version 1.8.9. He graduated from the primary school in Kolešovice, where his legendary nickname "Jaylub" also originated. He got the nickname because of his smell and because instead of normal food he eats his pigeons from his nose.
In his small circle of friends, there is also the well-known Jagr (Jan Bauler), who also makes fun of his smell behind his back, and he is also the only "normal" person who talks to him. He is currently studying at a secondary school in Podbořany, and as his old friend, I hope they also call him Jaylub at high school.

Averadge Day

He wakes up at approximately 8:00 and goes outside, yes without any hygiene. He always keeps the clothes he wore from the evening on and leaves in them in the morning. The clothes can be 3–5 months old.
At around 12:00, his mother calls him saying that breakfast is ready, so Jaylub goes home to eat breakfast and then goes outside again. As already mentioned, outside he mainly plays football and rides a bike. Sometimes he also walks his dog, but that is usually later in the evening.
At around 16:00, Jaylub’s mother calls again saying that lunch is ready, so Jaylub goes home to eat. When he goes out after lunch, it is already quite late or even dark, and nobody wants to go outside with him anymore. Therefore, Jaylub stays in his garden and plays with a ball. When it gets late (somewhere between 19:00–20:00), he goes home and plays on the computer the mentioned version of Minecraft 1.8.9, which he considers the only good version of Minecraft. Occasionally (more in the past, not anymore), he used to go on Discord servers to talk while playing with his former friends.
At 22:00, his mother calls him for dinner, so Jaylub goes to eat again. After dinner, he goes to sleep again without any hygiene, and as already mentioned, he still wears the same clothes.

Experiences

For most people, the only experience with Jaylub is that he smells, but some people have more experiences with him, such as sleepovers, visits, or playing football. Here are the experiences of anonymous people.
Visits
When you go to Jaylub’s house, you first have to pass through gates that are still locked with a large stone in front of them. When you pass through these gates, you will see his "small" yard, where it already smells quite strongly. In his yard there are, for example, free-roaming chickens, a dog locked in a small cage, a small barn, and a workshop in which there are many “treasures.” The chickens and the dog freely defecate in the yard, and nobody cleans it up, which is probably why the yard smells so much.
When you enter his house, you will see 3 main corridors. On the left there is a bathroom, which is unused. Its visual condition is terrible—for example, you can see bricks in the walls, there is no light or windows. In anonymous descriptions, nobody mentioned any visible shower, which may be why Jaylub smells so much.
The right corridor consists of a living room, where there is a small TV with an old Xbox 360 console, a dining table, a small sofa, and an aquarium that probably contains small fish. However, the living room is not very nice and has its flaws, such as the floor, which has probably never been vacuumed or cleaned. Some visitors regret not bringing slippers.
In the living room there is also one of the kitchens, which is fairly well equipped. It contains, for example, a microwave, a stove with an extractor hood and an oven. However, like the living room, the kitchen has probably never been vacuumed or cleaned. There is also a fridge that serves as the entrance to the kitchen, because opening it completely blocks access to the room.
If you go straight up the stairs in Jaylub’s house, you get to a relatively normal part of the house. The second floor contains all the rooms: two toilets (one probably non-functional and without doors), a second kitchen, and a balcony. The toilet is normal and clean.
Next to the toilet is Jaylub’s room. In Jaylub’s room there is a big mess. He puts everything he doesn’t know where to put under the bed. It is almost impossible to walk normally in the room, as you constantly have to avoid things. The room also contains boxes for clutter and his gaming setup.
His gaming setup, on which he only plays the mentioned version of Minecraft, consists of an old laptop, a TV, and a cheap mouse and keyboard from the brand Rapture. There is also a lot of mess on the desk. On the bed there is only a mattress with a pillow, and sometimes a blanket appears.
In the hallway of the second floor are the rooms of both sisters, but nobody knows what they look like, so these rooms cannot be described. The hallway does contain some mess, but the floor is relatively clean.
In the parents’ room, however, one of the visitors has been. The room is small and very messy, but it contains two beds and a light. The room does not contain much except for clutter and beds, and the mess is everywhere on the floor.
If we go back to the hallway and continue further, we arrive at his second kitchen. The second kitchen is slightly better equipped than the first one. It contains, like the first kitchen, a stove with an extractor hood, an oven, a microwave, a sink, a dining table, and also a “modern” TV. There is not much to describe about the second kitchen, as it is quite empty and normal.
If we continue further, we reach the balcony, where there is a grill and a view of the neighbor’s property.
Overall, his house is quite dirty. It has probably never been cleaned or vacuumed, and it would deserve a proper cleaning.
